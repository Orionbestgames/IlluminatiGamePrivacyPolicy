PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected within our game. We respect the privacy of our users, and our application has been designed to fully protect your data.

1. Collection of Personal Data

Our game does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data, including your name, email address, phone number, or precise location. Playing the game does not require creating an account or signing in through third-party services such as Google or Facebook.

2. Game Progress Storage (Local Data)

All game progress data, including completed levels, earned currency, purchased cards, upgrades, and language settings, is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. We do not have access to this data.

Please note that uninstalling the game or clearing the application's data through your Android device settings will permanently erase your saved progress.

3. Analytics and Advertising

Our game does not use any third-party tracking systems, analytics tools (such as Google Analytics), or advertising networks that monitor user behavior. The application does not display personalized advertisements.

4. Internet Access and Permissions

The game does not require an Internet connection to function properly and operates entirely offline. The application does not require or request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, private files, or any other sensitive device resources.

5. Children's Privacy

Because no user data is collected, the game is safe for children. The application does not collect age information and does not provide any means of interacting with other users online.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any updated version of this policy will be made available with future game updates.

7. Contact

As the game does not use servers or process user data, using the game constitutes acceptance of its free and privacy-focused nature.

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding the game, please contact the developer at: [orionbestgames@gmail.com](mailto:orionbestgames@gmail.com)
